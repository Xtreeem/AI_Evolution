
Classvaribles - Naming
	varible inside method		lowercase					ex. actor
	within class				underscore, lowercase		ex. _actor
	visable outside class		uppercase					ex. Actor

	When needing a classvarible utside class, create a get-property with 
	the same name skipping underscore and lowercase.

ClassMethods
	Use underscore to mark different words					ex. Recovery_Phase
	Use camelbacking except for in prepositions(for,to,of)	ex. Roll_for_Initiative
